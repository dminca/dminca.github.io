About

What started as a curiosity to decompose everything as a child, materialised itself into the first computer I owned. That’s when I was continously breaking and fixing it multiple times made me discover how everything works.

I’m Daniel, a Site Reliability Engineer working @ GfK SE based in Germany, let me invite you into my world ✨

My experience 😎

The first real IT job I landed was a Ruby on Rails developer at MarketsWorld which was a trading platform based in Isle of Man 🇬🇧. This is where I mastered the hidden practices of git, gained XP in OOP programming with Ruby on Rails and also fell in love with the terminal (console) & Linux/UNIX and how I was promoted to an actual Sysadmin role.

After gaining some more XP with Linux/UNIX in general, getting used with the command line utilities et. al., I’ve received an offer to join Gameloft, a gaming company based in Montreal 🇨🇦 as a DevOps Engineer.

99 % of most complicated problems have the most simplest solution

Here I’ve participated in quite some amazing projects, even helped create a Data Lake but also automated quite a lot of workflows in there via CI/CD pipelines. I’ve learned quite a lot from the wonderful people there and I’m still in touch with some of them even today. The quote cited, I lived it here and a colleague explained it to me in practice.

Little did I know that GfK SE 🇩🇪 was looking for a Site Reliability Engineer, I scored the technical interview and decided to relocate in 🇩🇪 with my wife and work with some amazing people.
